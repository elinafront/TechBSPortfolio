Baccalaureate
The baccalaureate examination is a degree completed at the end of upper secondary school, the purpose of which is to determine “whether students have acquired the knowledge and skills in accordance with the upper secondary school curriculum and sufficient maturity in accordance with the objectives of the upper secondary school.” In order to complete the degree, high school examinations are held twice a year in high schools, which the person who has passed and completed the high school curriculum is a student. -Wikipedia
Medical Laboratory Technician
Medical Laboratory Techician is an expert in clinical laboratory work who collaborates with other health care professional groups to produce the laboratory studies required for patient care and development, and to participate as a clinical laboratory expert in multidisciplinary teams. The Medical Laboratory Techician takes a variety of samples, guides patients and healthcare professionals, and conducts patient examinations as well as analyzes a variety of laboratory samples. The holistic management of the laboratory research process and an in-depth understanding of the factors involved and the quality management of the process form the basis of the Medical Laboratory Techician’s expertise. In addition to practical work, the Medical Laboratory Techician participates in the development of healthcare laboratory activities. -Metropolia
